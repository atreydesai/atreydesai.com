On his 20th birthday, Will Savage reflects on the meaning of "greatness" in the 21st century. He shares his personal journey from consumer AI software engineering to nuclear engineering, identifying the quest for abundant, carbon-free energy through fission as the "good quest" he chooses to dedicate his life to.
