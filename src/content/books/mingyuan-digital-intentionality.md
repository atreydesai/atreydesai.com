Mingyuan rebrands 'digital minimalism' as 'digital intentionality' to emphasize that it's not about avoiding technology, but about using tools with purpose. She shares the example of her partner, who is highly connected but remains in complete control of his devices, using them for learning and connection rather than passive consumption.
