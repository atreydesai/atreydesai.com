A fascinating profile of Jeff Dean and Sanjay Ghemawat, the legendary engineering pair who built much of Google's core infrastructure. The article details their unique pair-programming dynamic and describes the critical "war room" moments in 2000 when they saved the company by diagnosing a physical hardware corruption that was breaking the index.
