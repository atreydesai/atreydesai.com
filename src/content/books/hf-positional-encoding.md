A step-by-step walkthrough of the discovery of state-of-the-art positional encoding in transformer models, leading up to Rotary Positional Encoding (RoPE). It simplifies complex concepts to explain how we iteratively improve our approach to encoding position in modern LLMs like Llama 3.2.
