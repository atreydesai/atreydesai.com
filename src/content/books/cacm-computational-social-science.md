Hanna Wallach argues that computational social science should not be treated as simply applying machine learning or CS techniques to social data. Instead, it requires integrating social science perspectives, prioritizing transparency, interpretability, and rigorous error analyses, and using social science theories to drive the development of new computational techniques.
